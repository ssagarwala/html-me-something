My professional Experience: West Coast vs Midwest.

I am a graduate of San Jose State University. San Jose State University is in San Jose, CA. I did my Bachelor's in Computer Science back in 2005. After that I was working for some companies I also did some freelance work. I have learned alot of stuff on my own since I was making websites for clients. I learned alot of stuff on my own. Watching videos from youtube I moved for California to St. Louis, MO.

To me life in the midwest is very different than the life in the west. The life in the West coast is very fast as compared to the life over here. Especially the cooperate working environment is very different. When I started working, I was surprised to find out that my manager usually would not come to office until 10 and then leave around 11:30 for lunch. As compared to the west, escpecially California where managers usually sit till late.
